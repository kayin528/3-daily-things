2 daily things




January 1, 2019

It is the first day of a New Year! 2019 is going to be a year of continous self-improvement. Every day, I will be capturing 3 things that I've learned or did that enlightened me in some way. The road begins today and I can't wait to see where it leads!


1

Declare War On Yourself

What a gem this book is. I listened to it on the way back from my trip to Washington DC. This this helped me gain more self-awareness and has helped me realize to stay on top I must work even harder and declare war on myself. This book has given a great insight of what it really takes in order to get your act together.

2

The Panama Canal

On January 13, I will be headed to Panama City, Panama, the New York City of latin America. To prepare for my trip, I decided to go on youtube and see what vacationers were saying about their trip and experiences. I learned that the panama canal was the greatest infrastructure project the world has ever seen and took 10 years to complete with 22,000 casualties during construction.Today, the Panama Canal costs millions of dollars to get through.
